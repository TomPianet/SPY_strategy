Risk Management

These charts show how the strategy’s risk changes over time, so we can see if performance comes from steady returns or from taking big swings.

Rolling Volatility

Measures how much returns move up and down: the 30-day line shows recent risk, and the 252-day line shows the long-term risk level.

Rolling Max Drawdown

Shows the biggest drop from a recent peak during the last 252 trading days; more negative values mean the strategy had a deeper fall.

Rolling Sharpe Ratio

Shows risk-adjusted performance over the last 252 trading days; higher values mean the strategy earned more return for the risk it took.
